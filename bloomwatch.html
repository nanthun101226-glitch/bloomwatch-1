Welcome

This project focuses on how NASA investigates and monitors plant blooming in space and on Earth. It provides solutions for improving plant growth using technology and research.

About the Project

NASA has been experimenting with growing plants in space to support future space missions. Studying how plants bloom under zero-gravity, radiation, and controlled environments helps us find solutions for sustainable food production on Earth and beyond.
